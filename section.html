Abstract

The landscape of AI video generation is shifting towards fine-grained, controllable generation. We propose PISCO, a video diffusion model for precise video instance insertion with arbitrary sparse keyframe control.

PISCO allows users to specify sparse keyframes at arbitrary timestamps, and automatically propagates object appearance, motion, and interaction while maintaining physical consistency.
